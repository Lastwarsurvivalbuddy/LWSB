Common Questions

Built Different.

The questions people ask before they sign up — answered.

Why isn't this free?

Every answer Buddy gives costs real compute. We could slap ads on it — we're not going to. Ads mean sponsors. Sponsors mean incentives to tell you to buy things. That's the opposite of what Buddy is for.

What you're paying for is an AI that has zero reason to steer you wrong. No brand deals. No affiliate links. No "featured pack" arrangements. Just the best answer for your specific situation.

The free tier exists so you can see it's real. After that, the math is simple: one bad pack purchase costs more than a month of Pro.

Why not just use ChatGPT for Last War advice?

Go ask ChatGPT what you should focus on today in Last War. It'll give you a decent generic answer that applies to roughly nobody.

Buddy knows you're HQ 28 on Server 2412, Day 83, running Aircraft with T9 troops, and Enemy Buster in 3 days. ChatGPT doesn't know any of that. It can't. It has no idea what server day it is, what phase Arms Race is in, or what your squad power looks like.

Generic AI gives generic answers. Buddy gives yours.

Is my Last War game account safe?

Yes. Buddy never asks for your game login, your account credentials, or any access to your account whatsoever. Everything is self-reported — you tell us your HQ level, server day, troop type, and goals. That's it. No scraping. No account linking. No risk.

What if Last War: Survival Buddy gets something wrong?

Buddy will tell you when it doesn't know something rather than guess. That's a design decision, not a disclaimer — the Honesty Doctrine is baked into every response.

When Buddy hits a gap, it flags it and points you to TeachBuddy so you can contribute what you know. Every gap reported makes the platform smarter for every commander on it.

We're not building the illusion of omniscience. We're building something that gets better every day.
